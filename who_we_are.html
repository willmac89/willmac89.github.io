Who we are

Ian Macfarlane has over thirty years’ corporate finance and advisory experience - at Schroders, Swiss Bank Corporation (London and New York), Enskilda Securities (London and Helsinki), Europa Partners and Mirabaud Securities - before founding Luberon Capital Limited in 2017. He has advised on IPOs and on M&A transactions for listed and unlisted UK and international companies ranging from FTSE 100 companies to family-owned SMEs. He has a MA (Hons.) in Classics from the University of Oxford and speaks fluent French.

Elizabeth Jordan has over ten years’ experience in M&A at Europa Partners and Mirabaud Securities before joining Luberon Capital Limited in 2017. She has advised on a broad range of M&A transactions for both listed and unlisted UK and international companies She has a BSc (Hons.) in Geography from the University of Nottingham
