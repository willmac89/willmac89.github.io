We provide corporate finance advisory services to businesses facing structural change. This may include the sale or acquisition of a subsidiary, the raising of equity capital, or the sale of a whole company. We act as trusted advisers to repeat clients going back over decades. We advise large multinationals, private equity portfolio companies, SMEs and family-owned businesses. Sectors of special interest are Financial Services, Industrials and Business Services. Experience over the last thirty years includes M&A and equity capital market transactions in the UK, the Nordic Countries, France, Germany, Italy, India, North America and South East Asia. Services include advice relating to M&A, valuation, strategic reviews and raising equity.
